About Chibi URL

Chibi URL is a user-friendly and efficient URL Shortener service that simplifies the process of transforming long and complex URLs into shorter and more manageable links. With its robust technology stack including Django, Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and PostgreSQL, Chibi URL offers a reliable and feature-rich solution.

Key Features

  • URL Shortening
  • Index Your URL to easily Find Them
  • Search Functionality
  • User Authentication to Store Your URL's
  • Secure Storage with PostgreSQL
  • Card-style UI
  • Copy to Clipboard, Visit URL, and Delete Button in each URL Cards
  • Times Clicked Tracker with each Short Link

Experience the convenience of Chibi URL today and streamline your URL sharing process effortlessly, while maintaining control over your stored links and enjoying a visually appealing user interface.
